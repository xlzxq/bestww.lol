About Us

BestWW is a personal friend group maintaining over 70+ people. We were created back in February 2024 and have been going strong since. We are prominent in games, specifically Minecraft and Rocket League. Our name idea (BestWW) was created out of 3 things, firstly the people are known to be the best in certain games, secondly BestWW was already a thing in Minecraft PVP so it seemed suitable, and lastly we are all from different countries and regions.
